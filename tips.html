There are alot of different things that you can do!

  • Try to understand the basic principles
    • Why is it like this
    • When is this a useful calculation/note to keep in mind
    • Make mindmaps or lists, and start to test yourself if you can link or find relationships between different calculations/factors
    • Search up anything that you don't understand! There is a whole world out there with videos describing what you might not fully understand yet
